Phase 3 Trial of Semaglutide in Metabolic Dysfunction–Associated Steatohepatitis

In this phase 3 trial of 1,197 patients with biopsy-defined MASH and fibrosis stage 2 or 3, once-weekly subcutaneous semaglutide 2.4 mg significantly increased resolution of steatohepatitis without worsening of fibrosis (62.9% vs 34.3% with placebo) over 240 weeks, and improved fibrosis without worsening MASH.

1,197位切片證實的MASH合併中度纖維化患者隨機給予每週皮下注射semaglutide 2.4 mg或安慰劑240週。結果顯示semaglutide組脂肪肝炎緩解率達62.9%，明顯優於安慰劑組34.3%，且纖維化改善率亦較高，為MASH藥物治療奠定新基石。

NEJM 肥胖症治療

Orforglipron, an Oral Small-Molecule GLP-1 Receptor Agonist for Obesity Treatment

In a phase 3 trial enrolling 3,127 adults with obesity without diabetes, once-daily oral orforglipron led to mean weight reductions of −7.5% (6 mg), −8.4% (12 mg), and −11.2% (36 mg) at 72 weeks, versus −2.0% with placebo. Adverse events were predominantly gastrointestinal and dose-dependent.

肥胖且未合併糖尿病者隨機分配每日口服orforglipron(6/12/36 mg)或安慰劑72週。各劑量組體重分別下降7.5%、8.4%、11.2%，明顯優於安慰劑組的2.0%。副作用以腸胃道為主、呈劑量相關，為首個口服小分子GLP-1取得正面數據。

NEJM 第二型糖尿病

Orforglipron, an Oral Small-Molecule GLP-1 Receptor Agonist, in Early Type 2 Diabetes

Phase 3 double-blind, placebo-controlled trial assigned diet-and-exercise-only T2D patients to orforglipron 3/12/36 mg or placebo once daily for 40 weeks. HbA1c reductions were dose-dependent and significantly greater than placebo, with parallel weight loss—suggesting an oral GLP-1 option for early T2D management.

僅以飲食運動控制的早期第二型糖尿病患者隨機分配口服orforglipron(3/12/36 mg)或安慰劑40週。各劑量組HbA1c降幅與體重下降皆顯著優於安慰劑且呈劑量相關，提供早期糖尿病一個新的口服GLP-1選擇，有望改變治療階梯。

Global burden of metabolic dysfunction-associated steatotic liver disease, 1990–2023, and projections to 2050

Systematic GBD 2023 analysis showed global MASLD prevalence of 16.1% in 2023 (~1.3 billion cases). By 2050 prevalence is projected to rise 42%, reaching 1.8 billion, with the steepest increases in Asia. The data underscore urgent need for screening pathways, lifestyle intervention and access to newly approved pharmacotherapies.

The Lancet MASH治療策略

Current and emerging therapeutic landscape for metabolic dysfunction-associated steatohepatitis

This review summarises the recent shift from no licensed therapies to two approved agents—resmetirom (THR-β agonist) and semaglutide—for MASH with fibrosis. Authors outline patient selection, monitoring with non-invasive tests (FIB-4, ELF, vibration-controlled elastography) and the pipeline of FGF21 analogues, PPAR agonists and combination strategies.

JAMA 大腸癌篩檢

Population Health Colorectal Cancer Screening Strategies in Adults Aged 45 to 49 Years: A Randomized Clinical Trial

This randomized trial compared population-health outreach strategies (mailed FIT, mailed FIT plus colonoscopy invitation, and usual care) for boosting colorectal cancer screening uptake among adults aged 45–49 years. Both proactive outreach arms significantly increased screening completion versus usual care, supporting earlier implementation of organised CRC screening in this newly eligible age group.

本隨機試驗比較三種族群健康介入(郵寄FIT、郵寄FIT合併大腸鏡邀請、常規照護)在45–49歲族群中提升大腸癌篩檢率的效果。兩種主動介入皆顯著高於常規照護，支持台灣可比照美國USPSTF建議將篩檢年齡前移並採取主動寄發策略，提升年輕族群早期偵測率。

JAMA 大腸癌篩檢

Clinical Validation of a Cell-Free DNA Blood-Based Test for Colorectal Cancer Screening

Prospective pivotal study in 48,995 average-risk adults aged 45–85 evaluating a cell-free DNA blood test against screening colonoscopy. The cfDNA test detected colorectal cancer with sensitivity around 83% and advanced adenoma sensitivity remained modest (~13%), suggesting blood-based screening can lower entry barrier but is not yet a substitute for endoscopic detection of pre-cancerous polyps.

前瞻性樞紐研究納入48,995位45–85歲平均風險族群，評估血液 cfDNA 檢測對比大腸鏡篩檢。對大腸癌偵測敏感度約83%，但對進階腺瘤的敏感度僅約13%。顯示血液檢測可降低受檢門檻，但目前仍無法取代大腸鏡在偵測癌前病灶的角色，臨床溝通時應同時說明侷限。

AGA Living Clinical Practice Guideline on the Pharmacologic Management of Moderate-to-Severe Crohn's Disease

Updated AGA living guideline integrates evidence from newly approved biologics and small molecules (anti-TL1A, JAK inhibitors, S1P modulators) and recommends early advanced therapy over accelerated step-up for moderate-to-severe Crohn's disease. The panel emphasises shared decision-making, treat-to-target with mucosal healing, and routine therapeutic drug monitoring.

AGA 持續更新版指引整合近期核可的生物製劑與小分子藥(anti-TL1A、JAK抑制劑、S1P調節劑)，主張中重度克隆氏症應「早期使用進階治療」優於緩步升階。重點包括共享決策、以黏膜癒合為治療目標(treat-to-target)，並常規施行治療性藥物濃度監測(TDM)。

Global Consensus Recommendations for Metabolic Dysfunction-Associated Steatotic Liver Disease and Steatohepatitis

Multisociety global Delphi consensus establishes unified diagnostic terminology (MASLD/MASH), risk stratification with FIB-4 and elastography, and care pathways spanning primary care, endocrinology and hepatology. The consensus standardises non-invasive testing thresholds, defines audit metrics, and incorporates resmetirom and incretin-based therapy into stepwise management.

多學會全球德爾菲共識統一 MASLD/MASH 診斷詞彙，建立 FIB-4 與彈性影像分層流程，並橫跨家醫、內分泌與肝膽科的照護路徑。共識統一非侵入性檢測截值、定義稽核指標，並將 resmetirom 與 GLP-1 類藥物納入階梯式治療，是台灣建立脂肪肝照護網的重要參考。

Hepatology B型肝炎治癒

Hepatitis B Functional Cure: Current and Future Perspective

Comprehensive review summarises HBV functional cure (sustained HBsAg loss) achievement rates with current nucleos(t)ide analogues (3–5% over 10 years) and pegylated interferon (8–14%). Pipeline therapies including siRNAs (bepirovirsen, JNJ-3989), ASOs, capsid assembly modulators and therapeutic vaccines combined with NUC backbones show promising HBsAg reductions exceeding 90% in selected patients.

Hepatology 脂肪肝相關肝硬化

Characteristics and Clinical Outcomes in Patients With Cirrhosis Due to MASLD in Sweden

Nationwide Swedish register cohort of 2,318 compensated and 1,339 decompensated MASLD-cirrhosis patients (2001–2020) showed 5-year overall mortality of 28% and 60% respectively. HCC incidence was 1.4% per year in compensated cirrhosis. Type 2 diabetes and obesity independently predicted decompensation, supporting aggressive metabolic management even after cirrhosis develops.

瑞典全國登錄世代納入2,318名代償期與1,339名失代償期 MASLD 肝硬化患者(2001–2020)，五年總死亡率分別為28%與60%；代償期患者每年肝癌發生率1.4%。糖尿病與肥胖為失代償獨立危險因子，提示即使已肝硬化仍需積極代謝介入，呼應台灣脂肪肝病人需同步管控代謝症候群。
